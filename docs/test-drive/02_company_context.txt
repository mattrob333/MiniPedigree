HARBOR PEAK SUPPLY CO. — RAW COMPANY CONTEXT
(Paste this whole file into Company Context -> "Raw company context", then Parse Notes / Save.)

What we do: Harbor Peak Supply Co. sells curated outdoor gear (packs, shelters, trail kitchens)
through our own storefront and two wholesale channels. We design about a third of our catalog
and source the rest from twelve vendors. Roughly 60% of revenue is DTC, 40% wholesale.

Market: DTC outdoor enthusiasts in North America plus ~80 independent retail shops on
wholesale terms. Average DTC order $140; average wholesale PO $4,200.

Competitors: GearTrade, Backwoods Collective, and the big-box outdoor chains.

Goals:
1. Reach $6M revenue this year (currently tracking $4.8M run rate)
2. Cut order-to-ship time from 3 days to 1 day
3. Grow wholesale to 50% of revenue without adding headcount
4. Keep return rate under 6%

Initiatives: ShipStation automation rollout (Q3); wholesale portal pilot; vendor scorecard program.

Bottlenecks: Purchase orders pile up on Lena every Monday — reorder points live in her head.
Wholesale invoicing is manual and Maya loses two days a month reconciling Stripe payouts against
QuickBooks. Support tickets spike after every product drop and Ryan triages them by hand.
Marcus writes the weekly pipeline summary himself every Friday from HubSpot exports.

Systems: HubSpot (CRM), NetSuite (inventory + purchasing), ShipStation (fulfillment),
Zendesk (support), QuickBooks (accounting), Stripe (payments), Google Workspace, Slack,
Notion (SOPs), Canva, Google Analytics.

SOPs:
- SOP-3 Order exceptions: any order stuck >24h gets flagged in #ops-exceptions with owner and reason.
- SOP-8 Vendor reorder: reorder when stock cover falls below 21 days; POs above $5,000 need Sofia's sign-off.
- SOP-11 Support escalation: refund requests above $200 escalate to Priya; warranty claims always get a human reply.

Approval rules:
- Purchases above $5,000 must be approved by Sofia Reyes.
- Discounts above 20% require Marcus Bell's approval; above 30% the CEO signs off.
- Customer refunds above $200 require Priya Nair's sign-off and must be logged in the refund register.
- No AI-drafted email may be sent to a customer or vendor without human review.

Segregation of duties:
- The same person may not both create and approve a purchase order.
- The same person may not both issue a customer refund and approve it.

Compliance / security notes: Customer payment data lives only in Stripe. Customer PII must
never leave Zendesk or HubSpot.

KPIs (department · metric · cadence):
- Executive · Revenue run rate · monthly
- Revenue · Pipeline coverage (3x) · weekly
- Sales · Stale-deal count · weekly
- Marketing · DTC conversion rate · weekly
- Operations · Order-to-ship time (days) · weekly
- Operations · Stock cover (days) per top-50 SKU · weekly
- Customer Experience · First-response time (hours) · weekly
- Customer Experience · Return rate (%) · monthly
- Finance · Days to close the books · monthly

Terminology: stock cover, drop (product launch), trail kit, PO (purchase order),
payout reconciliation, exception lane.
